SIYPAONAC

(Sign into your personal account on a chromebook)

Requirements

A dysfunctional school IT team, more than one chromebook

Have Chrome open on the chromebook before starting!

Steps

1. Go to https://myaccount.google.com/security on a phone or device that isn't the one you're trying to sign in on

2. Scroll down to "Your devices" and click "Manage all devices"

3. Scroll to "X sessions on chrome devices" (Where X is a number)

4. Click on the chromebook you are signed into

5. Click "Sign out"

Continue all steps on the chromebook you want to have personal accounts on!

6. You should see a notification saying you've been signed out.

7. Go to https://tinyurl.com/addsession (redirects to chrome sign in, even if it's blocked)

8. Add your personal account

9. Repeat step 7, but this time add your school account

10. Profit

Persistence

If you signed out, turn off your wifi and click the sign in button. Press "Sign in with existing user".
